<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Cyber-Brutalist Minimalism**
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Raw Honesty**: Expose the structure; no hiding behind fluff.
  2. **High Contrast**: Stark black and white with neon accents.
  3. **Function over Form**: Every element has a clear purpose.
  4. **Speed**: Visuals that imply rapid movement and efficiency.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Base**: #000000 (Pure Black) and #FFFFFF (Pure White).
  - **Accent**: #CCFF00 (Neon Lime) - Represents raw energy and disruption.
  - **Reasoning**: High contrast demands attention and feels "tech-native" and urgent.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Asymmetric Grid**: Broken grids that challenge the eye but guide it logically.
  - **Overlapping Elements**: Text over images, images over borders to create depth.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Monospace Typography**: For code snippets and technical details.
  - **Visible Grid Lines**: Thin, faint lines separating sections.
  - **Glitch Effects**: Subtle visual glitches on hover to imply digital nature.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Instant Feedback**: Hover states are immediate and sharp.
  - **Scroll-Triggered Reveals**: Content snaps into place rather than fading.

  **Animation**:
  - **Hard Cuts**: Transitions are instant, no easing.
  - **Marquee Text**: Scrolling text for dynamic energy.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: **Space Grotesk** (Bold, geometric).
  - **Body**: **JetBrains Mono** (Technical, readable).
</idea>

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Base**: #0F172A (Deep Slate) to #1E293B (Slate).

  - **Reasoning**: Violet represents wisdom and future tech; gradients add a premium, magical feel.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Floating Cards**: Content contained in translucent cards.
  - **Z-Axis Layering**: Use depth to organize information hierarchy.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Blurred Backgrounds**: Backdrop-filter blur for glass effect.
  - **Glowing Borders**: Subtle gradients on borders to catch the "light".
  - **Mesh Gradients**: Soft, moving color blobs in the background.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Parallax**: Elements move at different speeds to enhance depth.
  - **Glow on Hover**: Elements light up when interacted with.

  **Animation**:
  - **Smooth Easing**: All movements are slow and fluid (ease-in-out).
  - **Float Animation**: Continuous gentle floating for key elements.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: **Clash Display** (Elegant, high contrast).
  - **Body**: **Satoshi** (Clean, modern sans).

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Swiss International Style (Digital Evolution)**
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Grid Precision**: Strict adherence to a mathematical grid.
  2. **Typography as Image**: Large, bold type is the main visual.
  3. **Objective Clarity**: Information is presented without decoration.
  4. **Negative Space**: Massive margins to let content breathe.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Base**: #F5F5F7 (Off-White) and #1D1D1F (Rich Black).
  - **Accent**: #FF3B30 (International Orange) or #007AFF (Swiss Blue).
  - **Reasoning**: Classic, timeless, authoritative. Feels like a high-end tool.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Modular Grid**: Content blocks align perfectly.
  - **Typographic Hierarchy**: Size and weight dictate importance, not color.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Huge Type**: Headlines that span the full width.
  - **Rule Lines**: Thick, black horizontal lines separating sections.
  - **Geometric Shapes**: Simple circles or squares as accents.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Micro-Interactions**: subtle shifts in position or color.
  - **Sticky Headers**: Navigation stays fixed for utility.

  **Animation**:
  - **Slide Up**: Content reveals by sliding up from behind a mask.
  - **Staggered Reveal**: Text lines appear one by one.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: **Helvetica Now Display** (or Inter Tight) - Iconic, neutral.
  - **Body**: **Inter** (Highly legible, standard).
</idea>
