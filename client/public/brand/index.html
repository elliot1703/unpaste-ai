[007] Voice & Tone

LANGUAGE RULES

Headlines

Use

"Stop Copy-Pasting."

"36 Hours → 1 Hour. Here's How."

"Your Website Should Work While You Sleep."

Never

"Leveraging Cutting-Edge AI Solutions"

"Your Digital Transformation Journey Starts Here"

"Committed to Excellence in AI-Powered Innovation"

Describing What We Do

Use

"AI that generates your quotes" — specific

"Connects your existing tools" — mechanical

"Works 24/7" — concrete outcome

Never

"AI-powered solution" — vague prefix

"Seamless integration" — nothing is seamless

"Revolutionising workflows" — we automate admin

Sentence Rhythm

Short. Declarative. We favour fragments over run-ons.

Paragraphs are 1–3 sentences. When we use a longer sentence, it's for technical explanation, not decoration.

The rhythm is: short punch, short punch, slightly longer explanation. Then back to punching.

Banned Words & Phrases

leverage synergy holistic trusted partner cutting-edge seamless revolutionise digital transformation solution just

Words We Use

smart website automation workflow waste fix build hours saved Efficiency Score Brisbane copy-paste bottleneck AI-native grow online
